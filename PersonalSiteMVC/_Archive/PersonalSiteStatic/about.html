About Me

Romeo Bagunu

Hey there! My name is Romeo, and for the past couple years, I have been working in child care as a Program Director. I learned some key professional skills as a manager and a supervisor, like teamwork, problem solving, and communication. I loved working with kids so much, but I hit a ceiling in my career there.
In my search for new opportunities, I realized that I could take a longtime hobby and interest in technology into a passion and a career in that field of work. That's when I was referred to Centriq's Full Stack Web Developer program, where I have deeply enjoyed learning languages in the .NET Stack and immediately applying them to projects.
